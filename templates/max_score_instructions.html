Instructions

This is a game where you are trying to get as many points as possible, by selecting vertical bars with the greatest combined height.

At the beginning of the game, the height of each bar is hidden.
Once you select a bar, its height will be revealed.
You select a bar by clicking on it twice. The first time you click, it will be outlined in red.
Click again to submit your selection. You will earn points equal to its height, added to your total score.
You can select the same bar multiple times

You will have {{trials}} chances to select bars and increase your score.

Bonus

You will receive a bonus proportional to the total number of points you earn in the game, up to $0.75.
For example, if you earn 2/3 of the maximum possible number of points for the game, you will receive 2/3 of $0.75, or $0.50.
If your performance is worse than it would be by random guessing, your HIT may be rejected.


Press Space to begin.
