Instructions

This is a game where you are trying to get as many points as possible, by finding the vertical bar with the greatest height.

At the beginning of the game, the height of each bar is hidden.
Once you select a bar, its height will be revealed.
You select a bar by clicking on it twice. The first time you click, it will be outlined in red.
Click again to submit your selection. You will earn points equal to its height, added to your total score.
You can select the same bar multiple times

The picture below shows an example with three bars.

The player first selected the middle bar, which would be worth 100 points.
Next, the player selected the right bar, which would be worth 200 points.
Next, the player selected the left bar. It is not the highest bar, so the score does not change.
Your score is based only on the highest bar that you select.

If your performance is worse than it would be by random guessing, your HIT may be rejected.
